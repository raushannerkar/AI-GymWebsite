About Muscle Matrix

Transform your fitness journey with Muscle Matrix, where technology meets guidance to propel you towards your goals. Our platform offers advanced features to enhance your experience.

1) Rep Counting & Form Correction: Using computer vision & ML, Muscle Matrix accurately counts reps & analyzes your form for various exercises like Bicep Curls, Pullups, & more. Get real-time feedback to optimize your workout.

2) Interactive Chatbots: Personalized routines & playlists ensure every workout suits your needs. Our chatbots offer support, motivation, & guidance throughout your fitness journey.

3) Fitness Calculator: Gain insights into your body & nutrition needs. Input details for personalized recommendations including BMR, Ideal Body Weight Range, & Nutrition Intake. Understand your TDEE effortlessly.

At Muscle Matrix, we're dedicated to empowering you. Join us today and redefine what's possible in your fitness journey.
